- **Representation**
  - Each component is a float vector **a** which translates to binary via sigmoid; its rank-1 tensor is the symmetric power **\(a^{{\otimes d}}\)**.

- **Search Moves**
  - Use **multiple initializations** to improve exploration.
  - Develop **heuristics** for stronger initial guesses.
  - Propose new methods for new factor guesses .

- **Signals to Optimize**
  - Minimize the **residual Hamming weight** (mismatched bits).

- **Rank Pressure**
  - When multiple exact solutions exist, prefer the one with **smaller rank** and **simpler components**.

- **Optimization**
  - Expect **many local minima** and **barren plateaus**; diversify restarts.
  - Tune **optimizer hyperparameters** and schedules.
  - Shape the **loss** to improve the landscape and convergence.
  - Choose the **number of steps** to balance solution quality and runtime.

- **Termination**
  - Stop when the residual is **exactly zero** and no single add/remove/edit can further reduce rank or improve correctness.
  - Otherwise, stop when a predefined **early-stopping criterion** (time, steps, or plateau) is met.
